HOW WE THINK

Four principles of honest validation.

01

Guilty until proven

Every apparent edge is assumed false until it survives the full gauntlet. The burden of proof sits on the strategy, never on the doubt.

02

Correct for the search

The luckiest of ten thousand configs is expected to look brilliant by pure chance. We deflate the result by how hard you searched.

03

Costs are not a footnote

Spread and fees quietly decide more strategies than signals do. Every edge is re-priced at your real cost before it earns a verdict.

04

Performance over source

Validity lives in how a strategy behaved on data — never in how elegant its code reads. We judge the trades, not the prose.

WHAT IT CHECKS

Six ways a backtest fools you.

Each strategy is run through the same gauntlet a serious quant desk would apply — and most apparent edges do not survive it.

01

Overfitting (Deflated Sharpe)

Corrects the apparent edge for how many configs you searched. The single biggest reason a backtest winner dies live.

02

Sample size

A great result on a handful of trades is noise wearing a costume. We put a confidence interval on the per-trade edge.

03

Cost survival

Spread and fees quietly eat thin edges. We compute the break-even fee and the net edge at your real cost.

04

Regime robustness

An edge that only works in a trending market is fragile. Split across up, down, and chop windows. (Needs per-trade timestamps.)

05

Look-ahead / leakage

Strategies that secretly use future data look perfect and are worthless. Flagged from the trade structure.

06

Locked holdout

The cleanest test: measure the edge only on data that played no part in choosing the strategy.

THE GAUNTLET

How a strategy earns trust.

An edge is presumed false until it survives every stage. Most are eliminated at stage one.

DEFAULT // GUILTY
Burden of proof is on the strategy.
01
Stage 01

Leakage audit

Confirm the strategy never touched information it could not have had at decision time. Future data is the most common silent killer.

02
Stage 02

Cost & capacity stress

Re-price every trade with realistic spread and fees. Edges that need free execution are removed here.

03
Stage 03

Purged cross-validation

Test on time-separated folds so overlapping trades cannot leak between training and testing.

04
Stage 04

Deflated Sharpe haircut

Discount the result by the number of configurations searched. The luckiest of thousands is expected to look brilliant.

05
Stage 05

Placebo / falsification

Run the same logic on shuffled or random data. A real edge disappears; an artifact persists.

06
Stage 06

Locked holdout

A final verdict on data sealed away from the entire selection process. Survive this and the edge is credible.
